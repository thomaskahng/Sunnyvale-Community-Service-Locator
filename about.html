Local Community Service Find My Service

This website was created by two high school students who wanted to help other students find a faster way to find places to volunteer. The creators, Sam Abraham & Thomas Kahng, are both students at Sunnyvale High School. The idea for this website started when they both had an idea to create a website for their High School Project. This website is meant to be used by people, mainly students, who are looking for places to volunteer. Many students must have a minimum requirement of volunteering hours or community service hours. This website is meant to make the search for those locations easier and faster than traditional searching.
